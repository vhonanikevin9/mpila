What is Goods In Transit Insurance?

We understand the critical importance of safeguarding your valuables throughout their journey. Our Goods in Transit Insurance offers a robust and tailored solution to mitigate risks associated with the transportation of goods.


Whether it's a short-distance delivery or long-distance delivery, our insurance coverage is designed to protect your valuable cargo from potential damage, loss, or theft.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a removal service?

Simply fill out the "Request a Quote" form on our website with your details and inventory, or contact us directly via WhatsApp or Phone at 0734053920. We will respond promptly to arrange your booking.

What vehicles are available for moving?

We maintain a flexible fleet tailored to your needs, including 1 Ton, 1.5 Ton, 3 Ton, and 8 Ton Bakkies, suitable for everything from single-room moves to full 3-bedroom houses.

How does Goods in Transit (GIT) insurance work?

Our GIT insurance protects your cargo against potential damage, loss, or theft while on the road, providing you with total peace of mind for both short and long-distance relocations.

Are your storage facilities secure?

Yes, our storage facilities are highly secure, clean, and monitored, ensuring your belongings are kept safe whether you require short-term holding or long-term archiving.
